Bowl of Oatmeal

Some oatmeal and few other tasty items

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup quick cooking oats
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • ¼ banana, mashed
  • ½ tablespoon and ¾ teaspoon flax seed meal
  • ½ tablespoon peanut butter

Steps:

  1. Whisk oats, milk, yogurt, banana, flax seed meal, and peanut butter together in a bowl. Refrigerate until set, at least 15 minutes.
